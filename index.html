Egbu, Owerri North

Egbu is a town in Owerri North Local Government Area of Imo State, Nigeria. It is known for its peaceful environment and strong cultural heritage. The town is close to Owerri city and is remarkable for some important facts. The Otammiri river begins in Egbu and the Bible was translated from English to Igbo in Egbu. Egbu is also known for agriculture and community development.
